About Tokerize

Tokerize is an independent community media focused on real-world asset tokenization — with a particular focus on T-RIZE and the RIZENET ecosystem.

Our goal is simple: make institutional-grade tokenization intelligence accessible to every member of the RIZE community, regardless of their background in finance or blockchain technology.

What We Cover

We publish in-depth analyses of tokenized instruments, structured finance mechanisms, on-chain governance models, and the broader tokenization landscape. Every article is written to be understood from first principles.

RIZE Data Hub

Beyond editorial content, Tokerize operates the RIZE Data Hub — a central gateway to four live applications. The RIZE Market Data Hub tracks RIZE price, market cap, TVS/TVL ratios, and comparative market positioning in real time. The RIZE Ecosystem Hub maps the full Rizenet and T-RIZE institutional ecosystem: Canton Network partners, Foundation board members, and live RWA deals. The CC Market Data Hub mirrors the same market intelligence experience for Canton Coin. The Canton Ecosystem Hub covers 283 Canton Network ecosystem participants, Canton Improvement Proposals (CIPs), and live on-chain governance votes powered by the 5N Lighthouse API.

Independence

Tokerize is community-driven and independently operated. We are not affiliated with T-RIZE Group. All content reflects our own analysis and research.
