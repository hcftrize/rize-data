Understand CC Tokenomics Influence

CC tokenomics shifted positively for T-RIZE: since Jan 2026, 62% of rewards go to app providers (~516M CC/month), and validator allocation rose from 12% to 18%. Yet T-RIZE validator revenue declined — because the CC burn/mint ratio is approaching equilibrium. More CC burned & less minted shrinks CC unit rewards, but supports CC price appreciation. Track these dynamics in the CC Market Data Hub.
